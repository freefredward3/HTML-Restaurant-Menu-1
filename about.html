Origin Story

In a land with nothing but Pizza, Burgers and Tacos. Oscar DelThai was tiered of eating the same food in his small home town of Lodi. He ventured into the land of business opportunity to bring diversification to his towns cuisine. In 1967 he opened his first Thai food restaurant “H-Thai-M-L” in Lodi and has been a successful business ever since!!
